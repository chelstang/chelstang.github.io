Connecting Worlds

Time span: 1 week
Program: Procreate

Individual

This piece was commissioned by the Global Languages and Cultures department at Carnegie Mellon University. In order to showcase connectivity through language, I created a fantasy city in which two cultures collide. The focal point features a language bridge that allows the inhabitants to explore, understand, and grow alongside their neighbors.

These drawings are the proposal sketches for the concept that developed into the final piecce.
