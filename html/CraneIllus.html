Whooping Crane Illustration

Time span: 3 weeks
Programs: Adobe illustrator, procreate

individual

This is a depiction of the whooping crane in their natural habitat using a limited color pallette. The beauty of the crane hunting is eerily met with the city skyline lerking behind the water grasses. The expansion of urbanization has lead to the loss of swamplands in Texas and Canada leading to the endagerment of this spieces. Viewers of the poster are encouraged to take action against habitat loss so that these birds can take flight once more.

Development of the illustration started with sketching thumbnails for ideas. Then, research on the specifics of the crane's habitat ensured accurate portrayal in the drawing.
