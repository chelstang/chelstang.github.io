Modular Structure

Time Span: 2 weeks
Materials: Chipboard, paint

individual

Two simple modules make up this whole structure. The modularity allows for flexibility and scalability, making it suitable for many environments. This specific model acts as a waiting space, and it is intended to reverse the negative emotions associated with waiting.

As passerbyers wait in the space, they can explore the complexities of the structure. Both the construction and the patterns are meant to facilitate movement to prevent bordem of it's inhabitants.
