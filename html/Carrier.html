Sustainable Flower Carrier

Time span: 3 weeks
Material: Cardboard

Individual

This carrier was specifically designed to carry flower bouquet in sustainable manner. It is completely made of recycable cardboard and constructable in 30 seconds from a single flat sheet. In addition to these features, the carrier can be converted to a vase that allows the flowers to be on display, extending the lifespan of the carrier and preventing additional waste.


Development and process

To convert the carrier from its mobile mode to its stationary mode, the handles simply need to be slotted into place on the sides.
